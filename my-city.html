My city Al Karak

Al-Karak is a historic city in Jordan, located about 140 km south of Amman. It is famous for its Crusader castle, Karak Castle, built in the 12th century by the Crusaders.

The city has a mix of Christian and Islamic history and offers stunning views of the Dead Sea and surrounding valleys. Today, it is a key cultural and tourist site, known for its rich heritage and traditional Jordanian cuisine.
