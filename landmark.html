Al Karak Castle

Al-Karak Castle (قلعة الكرك) is a historic Crusader fortress located in Karak, Jordan. Built in the 12th century by the Crusaders, it served as a strategic stronghold along the King’s Highway.

Today, it’s a popular tourist site with museums and panoramic views of the Dead Sea and surrounding valleys.

Location

Al-Karak Castle is located in the city of Al-Karak, Jordan, approximately 140 km south of Amman and 30 km north of Petra.

Why I Chose This Landmark

I chose Al Karak Castle because it's one of the largest & best-preserved Crusader castles in the Levant, it played a key role in Saladin’s conquests (Battle of Hattin, 1187) and it has massive walls, secret tunnels, and dark dungeons to explore.
